STORY

In a decayed world, they fight against a cursed destiny. Long ago, in the distant reaches of Land's End... The unforeseen descent of the "Rain" transformed all living things into frenzied undead monstrosities known as the Blighted. Faced with a calamity far beyond their understanding with no recourse, the kingdom fell into ruin, with no end in sight to the accursed rain. Meanwhile, in the remnants of this ruined world, a young girl named Lily awakens in the depths of a church.

GAMEPLAY

2D Action - Adventure RPG

"ENDER LILIES" is a dark fantasy 2D action RPG that sees the players try to unravel the mystery of the kingdom destroyed by the '"Rain of Death." Players will journey through the sprawling and hauntedly beautiful locations of "Land’s End," including a submerged forest, a sealed-off contaminated underground cavern, and a grand castle. In the many places you will visit on this sorrowful adventure, formidable bosses await that will gladly claim your life given even the slightest chance. Defeat these powerful foes and release them from their unending curse to turn them into allies. Overcome the challenges before you and search for the truth with powerful knights at your side.

Spirits

Lily is a frail, young girl. Her sole purpose is to fulfill the role of a White Priestess, as she alone has the power to perform the rite of Purification. Though unable to fight herself, Lily can call upon the Spirits of a whole host of immortal knights to do battle in her stead upon purifying their Blight-corrupted souls. Each Spirit possesses unique skills. A giant to blow foes away with a swing of their humongous hammer, for instance. Or a headless knight that unleashes a powerful counterattack when summoned in-time with an enemy attack. Others still fight with loyal beasts at their side while accompanying Lily. A vast number of skill variations are at your beck and call. Up to six different Spirits can be equipped and switched between in the heat of battle. Combine their skill effects to create your own combos! Utilize swords, magic, and the unique powers of 26 immortal Spirits to endure the harsh world of Land's End.

Enhancement & Equipment

Ender Lilies allows the player to enhance their Spirits and mix-and-match equipment called "Relics" to build the perfect combat style to suit their playstyle. Spirits can be enhanced by consuming Blighted "residue" found throughout Land's End. Enhancements can bestow various upgrades to Spirits, such as increasing the number of strikes in their melee combo or granting additional projectiles to their long-range attacks. Enjoy enhancing the skills of your favorite Spirits. Make sure and equip the many Relics you unearth along your journey. These curiosities will aid you in battle or while exploring Land's End. Their manifold boons may reshape the way you play the game.

Exprolation

The expansive and captivating kingdom of Land's End stretches out before you. Freely choose which direction to take and what path you will forge. The immortal knights make excellent traveling companions, as they help Lily traverse the landscape. Aerial dash, double jump, wall climb, and hook-swing your way to uncovering new areas. The more Spirits you purify, the more the world opens up!

CHARACTER

Lily

Our fated protagonist and sole survivor in a land the luster of which was long since tarnished. As a White Priestess, it will be her duty to purify the Blight that contaminates the world. Stirred from her slumber by the Umbral Knight, bewilderment sets in as she gazes across the terrible transformation the land has undergone. What will become of her? Is it her fate to regain her memories through her journey? Will she find among the tattered landscape that which will allow her to grow and be the salvation of the world? Or is her fate to become something else entirely...?

Umbral Knight

A knight in black garb not from the ruined kingdom. Though naught but a blighted spirit, they still retain some semblance of who they once were.
